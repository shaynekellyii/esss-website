Join us.

There are a number of ways to get involved. Isn't that awesome?


work
Take on a director position

Director positions are appointed by the ESSS executive council. Directors have smaller but important roles in the society and hold the position for one calendar year.

perm_identity
Run for an executive position

Every year in the spring, we hold elections for all VP positions (and a few others). Come to our AGM in March or drop by our office hours to talk to us about it.

today
Come to our meetings

If you're interested in what we do, just come out to one of our weekly meetings. Provided you meet our membership requirements, you'll be able to vote and have your say. This is how a number of our current executive got started with the ESSS.

thumb_up
Are you a first year?

Welcome! Within the first couple weeks of the year we'll hold first-year rep elections. You should step up and get involved. It's a great way to have your voice heard and to meet a bunch more cool people.

chat_bubble
Come to our events

This really is the most fun way to get involved. Seriously, who doesn't like a pub crawl?

business
Drop by our office

We have regular office hours. They're held in ASB 10868. We don't bite. If anything, ask for candy and we'll probably give you some.
