Movember Pub Crawl

Come out to the MSESS and ESSS' second annual Movember Pub Crawl. Tickets are $20 and include a free drink and cover to four locations.


We are going to start the night at Blarney Stone at 8:00 pm, head to Portside Pub at 9:00, Alexander Gastown at 10:15, and our final destination will be M.I.A at 11:30.


Come by the ESSS office during office hours or email social@esss.ca to get tickets.
