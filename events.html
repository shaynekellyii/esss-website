Annual events

Every year we tend to do these things. This doesn't guarantee we'll do any of them, but chances are you can expect them to happen.
Frosh week
September

First week of school, festivities for first-years including Midnight Madness and scavENG.

Photos
Pub crawl
November

We get people together and go to pubs and clubs together with sweet discounts. Fun times.

Photos
Polar Plunge
January or February

We toss people into the pond in the AQ for charitable donations to the Variety Children's Charity Network.

Photos
SFU Engineering Competition
October-ish

A competition to find SFU representatives to send to the Western Engineering Competition.

Photos
Western Engineering Competition
January

A competition against all engineering schools west of Ontario. More info at wec[InsertYearHere].com (e.g. wec2016.com).

Photos
Canadian Engineering Competition
March

The top two teams from each category at the Western Engineering Competition get to compete nationwide at CEC. If they win there, they can go on to the International Engineering Competition.
